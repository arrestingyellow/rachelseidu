About Rachel Seidu

Born in May 1997, Rachel Seidu is a Nigerian-Ghanaian photographer working out of Benin City, Nigeria. With family and roots in her hometown of Lagos, Seidu has been practicing photography professionally for the past two years and is currently also a student. As a true creative, while photography is currently her main mode of expression, she dabbles in anything and everything. That being said, what’s most striking about her photography work is her “up close and personal” capturing of her subjects. Most of her work is captured in black and white and often showcases the usage of double exposure editing.
